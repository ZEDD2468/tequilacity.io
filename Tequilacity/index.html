Tequila is the main city of the municipality with the same name in Jalisco, Mexico. It is located in the center-east side of the state and is famous for giving its name to the national drink, tequila. Since the 19th century, tequila production has been the town's primary economic foundation. Tequila is situated only 69 km away from Guadalajara, and visitors from Guadalajara frequently visit the city. Other state capitals near this "Pueblo Mágico" (Magic Town) include Tepic at 154 km, Aguascalientes at 285 km, Colima at 247 km, and Guanajuato at 336 km.
